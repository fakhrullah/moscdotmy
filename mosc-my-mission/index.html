MOSC MY Mission

Mon, Feb 13, 2017
The Malaysia Open Source Conference (MOSC MY) mission is to accomplish World Class Human Resources in Information Technology, through knowledge sharing and the usage of Open Source Software. MOSCMY is run by volunteers. As days go by we are rapidly increase momentum. This is the largest "**Teh Tarik Session**" for open source community in Malaysia.
